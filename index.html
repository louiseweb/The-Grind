Our Story

The Grind had humble beginnings. A family fun independent coffee shop founded in 1985, Mill Hill, North London. The Grind came about at a time of growing demand for high quality coffee products. Customers would come and enjoy the experience recommending to friends and family far and wide. Since then, we have gone from strength to strength.


The Grind has recently been acquired by the next generation of coffee connoisseur. Son and daughter team, Nick and Emma Harper, who have now taken the reigns of the family business. Their goal is to create a professional, quality brand which modernises the family business but still embodies its original values of being an inviting coffee shop which offers its customers quality and the personal touch that comes from an independent establishment.


Our Awards

The Grind were awarded 3 great taste awards, 2017, 2020 and 2022. This credits the business that has developed innovative, original, experimental or simply brilliant food and drink products, and who are sure to be the next big thing.

Our Coffee

We’ve defined all of our coffees by collections, tailored to your taste. Each collection represents an experience; starting with Espresso - your daily cup, then journey through our single origins and coffees that push boundaries all the way through to Rarities, limited editions from the world’s most acclaimed farms and producers.
